# Pricing

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Restricted** — For government partners and institutional procurement only.
{% endhint %}

***

Giga Meter pricing is structured on a cost-recovery basis. Fees cover the infrastructure, operations, and support required to run a sustainable measurement programme — not profit.

* No fees per school, per user, or per measurement
* No additional fees as your deployment scales
* Any surplus is reinvested in the product

***
